AI Cooking Timer

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Overview

AI Cooking Timer is designed to work without an account and without tracking. The app uses cooking photos and timer history to help you recognize active timers and suggest useful timer durations on your device.

Data stored on your device

The app may store the photos you take for timers, timer labels, durations, timer status, notification settings, and on-device learning history. This data is stored locally on your device and is used for app functionality.

Data collection

We do not collect, sell, or share your cooking photos, timer history, or personal data. The app does not require sign-in and does not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Notifications and widgets

The app uses local notifications to alert you when a timer finishes and WidgetKit to show timer information on your Home Screen. These features run through Apple system services on your device.

Purchases

If you buy AI Cooking Timer Premium, the purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. The app checks your purchase entitlement so it can unlock Premium features.

Your choices

You can delete timers in the app, clear learning history in settings, remove Home Screen widgets, and disable notifications in iOS Settings at any time.

Contact

For privacy questions, open a support request at GitHub Issues.
