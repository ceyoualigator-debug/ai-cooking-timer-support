AI Cooking Timer

Support

Need help with photo timers, notifications, widgets, or Premium? Start here.

Basic use

Open the app, tap the plus button, take a cooking photo, set the time, and start the timer. When several timers are running, the app sorts them by least time remaining.

Notifications

If timer alerts do not appear, make sure notifications are enabled for AI Cooking Timer in iOS Settings. The app uses local notifications, so no account or server is required.

Widgets

Add the AI Cooking Timer widget from the Home Screen widget gallery to keep the next timer visible outside the app.

Premium

The free version supports up to two active timers. AI Cooking Timer Premium is a one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited active timers. Purchases and restores are handled by Apple.

Contact support

Open a support request at GitHub Issues. Include your device model, iOS version, and what you were doing when the issue happened.
